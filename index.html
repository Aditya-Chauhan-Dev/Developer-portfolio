About Me

I'm Alex, a self-taught and highly motivated frontend developer with 5+ years of experience transforming complex requirements into elegant, interactive user interfaces. My primary focus is on performance, accessibility, and writing clean, maintainable code.

My journey began with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, giving me a strong foundation in the core web technologies.
